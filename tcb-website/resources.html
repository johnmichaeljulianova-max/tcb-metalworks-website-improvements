FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the questions we hear most. Don't see yours? Get in touch.

What types of metal fabrication does TCB Metal Works offer? +
We provide structural steel, stainless steel, welding, sheet metal fabrication, CNC plasma cutting, CNC horizontal machining, and 300-ton press brake forming — plus our flagship specialty, custom zoo and animal enclosures. See our Solutions for the full range.
Do you build custom, one-off projects? +
Yes. Every project is engineered to your exact specifications. Whether it's a single custom part, a structural build, or a complete animal enclosure, we take it from drawings or a concept through to a finished, installed result.
What makes your zoo and animal enclosures different? +
Our enclosures use TIG-welded aluminium and stainless steel for corrosion resistance and hygiene, with containment engineered around species behaviour, welfare, and public safety — built to institutional standards. Learn more on our Zoo Enclosures page.
What materials do you work with? +
Carbon and structural steel, stainless steel, and aluminium, among others. We'll recommend the right material for your project's strength, corrosion, hygiene, and finish requirements.
What areas do you serve? +
We're based at 1600 Layard Ave, Racine, WI, and serve clients throughout the region — and nationwide for specialized fabrication and enclosure work.
How do I get a quote? +
Send us your drawings or describe your project through our contact form, or call 224-766-9928. We typically respond within one business day.

Purpose-Built Enclosures

Enclosures Engineered for Animals, Safety & Longevity

Animal enclosures are unforgiving work — they demand precise containment, animal-safe materials, and finishes that hold up for decades. It's a discipline we've made our flagship, combining precision fabrication with a deep respect for animal welfare and public safety.

  • TIG-welded aluminium & stainless — corrosion-resistant and hygienic.
  • Engineered containment — designed around species behaviour and safety.
  • Built to institutional standards — for accredited zoos and research use.
  • Fully custom — one-off enclosures and exhibits, built to your spec.
